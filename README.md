## ⚡ About

Backend and data engineer in the making. Over the past 1.5 years I have shipped a **production multi-tenant SaaS backend** serving 1,000+ daily requests, a **live analytics platform for Irish public data**, and a **multi-agent AI system on Google Cloud Run**. First author of a **published paper** on real-time ML for accessibility.

Currently finishing my MSc at Dublin City University, where my practicum disaggregates 30-minute smart meter data into appliance-level usage and tells Irish households where their energy actually goes.
